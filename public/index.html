- How to use Moppy?

  • Add points crtl + left click.
  • How to add line?
    • choose the points by clicking on it by the middle mouse button.
    • then choose the other point and click on it by the middle mouse button.
  • Move canvas shift + left click.
  • How to add function?
    • open the side panel
    • type your function example: 1/x and x*x, x+5...
